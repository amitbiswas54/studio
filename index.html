Event Description

During this exclusive event we will discuss on the importance of sustainability in the digital workplace. As society changes, ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) concerns are top of the agenda for consumers, corporate decision makers, and regulators. Enterprises are increasingly aware that their appeal to customers, investors, and employees is intrinsically linked to their ability to demonstrate a commitment to creating an inclusive and sustainable society.

You will have the opportunity to sit together with peer CIOs, sustainability managers and experts to listen to their personal testimonials and exchange ideas on techniques and practices to help your organisation to deliver net zero outcomes.
